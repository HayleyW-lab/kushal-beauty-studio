Meet Jyoti

Passion for Beauty,
Care for People

Welcome to Kushal Beauty Studio — a place where beauty meets care. Founded by Supinder (Jyoti) Kaur, our studio has been a trusted destination for beauty treatments in the heart of Auckland CBD.

Growing up with a deep appreciation for beauty rituals and skincare traditions, Jyoti brought her expertise and passion to New Zealand — creating a warm, welcoming space where every client feels valued and leaves feeling their absolute best.

Whether it's perfectly shaped brows, a glowing facial, or silky smooth skin after a wax, Jyoti and her team take pride in delivering beautiful results with every single visit.
